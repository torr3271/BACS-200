1988.

What began as a way of improving the lives of former gang members in East Los Angeles has evolved into the largest gang intervention, rehab and re-entry program in the world. Each year we welcome thousands of people through our doors seeking to transform their lives.

Over 30 years.

The tide of gang activity in Los Angeles has turned, the field of re-entry services has broadened, and public safety has become more enlightened in ways that would not have been possible without the advocacy of Homeboy Industries.
